Hi! I'm Jiahui from PCALab, NJUST. I received my Ph.D in Nov. 2025, advised by Prof. Jian Yang and Prof. Beibei Wang. As a young Padawan in Graphics, I started my journey in realistic rendering since my first year of Ph.D in 2020. My main research interests are in the field of rendering, including neural appearance modeling and inverse rendering, as well as other close topics related to computer vision (such as relighting).

Apart from my research, I'm also deeply interested in modern dramas and movies (acting, screenwriting and directing), music theory, Formula 1 racing, TRPGs (scriptwriting and hosting), video games, learning foreign languages. and of course, making new friends!

What am I doing?

My research interests mainly lie in realistic rendering and deep learning, specifically:
  • Neural-based appearance modeling.

  • Lightweight BTF/SVBRDF acquisition.

  • Inverse rendering and reconstruction.
