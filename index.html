What am I doing?

My research interests mainly lie in realistic rendering and (plus) deep learning, specifically:
  • Neural-based appearance modeling.

  • Lightweight BTF/SVBRDF acquisition.

  • Efficient rendering of complex appearances (glints and layered materials).

  • Inverse rendering for materials and relighting.
